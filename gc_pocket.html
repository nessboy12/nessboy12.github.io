To update your device, hold the 'Update' button while plugging in your device.

Click the 'connect' button and follow the prompts.

Update 5.20.2023

Resolves USB Timeout breaking.

Adds negative acceleration scaling option.

Updated stick sensitivity to 'acceleration' (See configurator).

Added additional pollings on Non-Performance modes (1ms controller polling is now constant)

Changes from 4.27:

This update resolves the issue where two adapters conflict on Nintendo Switch.

The issue requires a fix to enable/disable 1ms polling or what I am now

referring to as 'Performance Mode'. Performance Mode is now disabled by

default and you can enable it by pressing both buttons at the same time

when the device is in standby (Red LED).

Reduces latency on Switch by ~3ms.
